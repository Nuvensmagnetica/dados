GPMCC - Research group in scientific and computational mathematics

The Research Group on Scientific and Computational Mathematics (GPMCC) is formed mainly by mathematicians from the Federal Institute of Education, Science and Technology of São Paulo in São José dos Campos and brings together researchers instigated to solve problems based on Mathematics. Thus, the research consists of different themes based on mathematical sciences, in general, using computing as an auxiliary tool. In addition, it seeks to bring science, education and productive arrangements together for regional development and scientific dissemination.
