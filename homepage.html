Characterization of magnetic clouds

About the search

This website comes from a research completed in December 2021, on the characterization of the behavior of magnetic clouds (MCs), through data obtained during the passage of interplanetary coronal mass ejections (ICMEs) by the Advanced Composition Explorer (ACE) spacecraft. in orbit at the Lagrangian point L1, that is, at the Earth-Sun gravitational equilibrium point of the interplanetary medium. The scientific initiation obtained results on the analysis of the characterization of the data provided by the ACE (1) satellite, Richardson and Cane (3) cataloging and the use of Oliveira (5) software for the processing of information consistent with the project and analysis of graphics for the determination of the flow tube of a magnetic cloud.
